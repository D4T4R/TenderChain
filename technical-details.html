System Architecture

Detailed breakdown of TenderChain's decentralized architecture and core components

1

Tender Creation

Government/Organization creates tender with specifications, evaluation criteria, and budget. All data is encrypted and stored immutably on blockchain.

2

Public Announcement

Tender details are automatically published on the platform with equal access for all bidders. Smart notifications alert relevant contractors.

3

Bid Submission

Contractors submit sealed bids using symmetric key encryption. Bids remain private until deadline, ensuring fairness and preventing manipulation.

4

Automated Evaluation

Smart contracts automatically evaluate bids against predefined criteria. Scoring is transparent, consistent, and free from human bias.

5

Milestone Creation

Selected contract is divided into milestones. Each milestone has specific deliverables, verification requirements, and payment schedules.

6

Verification & Payment

Two-step verification by domain experts and officials. Automatic payment release upon successful milestone completion.
